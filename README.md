🛒 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data using SQL, Python, and Power BI to generate meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.
The project covers the complete data analytics workflow including data extraction, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, business intelligence reporting, and dashboard development.
The objective is to identify sales trends, profitable products, customer purchasing behavior, and regional performance to help businesses improve profitability and operational efficiency.
________________________________________
🎯 Project Objectives
•	Analyze overall sales performance.
•	Identify top-performing products and categories.
•	Evaluate regional sales trends.
•	Understand customer segment behavior.
•	Measure business profitability.
•	Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
•	Generate actionable business recommendations.

🔍 Key Insights
•	Total Sales exceeded $2.30 Million.
•	Total Profit reached approximately $286 Thousand.
•	West Region generated the highest sales revenue.
•	Technology Category contributed the highest sales and profit.
•	Consumer Segment was the largest contributor to revenue.
•	Top-performing products accounted for a significant portion of sales.
•	Sales performance varied across regions and states.

💡 Business Recommendations
